Big Hit



Nehru Nagar, Thuraipakkam, Chennai 600097

Big Hit Restaurant offers both family dining and drinks. Enjoy exotic Veg and Non-Veg food. It is the perfect place to crash with your friends / family and enjoy your favorite food while enjoying your favorite cricket/football match!

Huge place with indoor seating and also open air roof top seating with sheesha lounge. Inside ambience will delight you.

We have both alacarte menu and Buffet menu. Do check the restaurant for their buffet timings and menu.

The Vintage Kitchen



103, Nungambakkam High Rd, Chennai, 600034

The Vintage Kitchen excellence in family dining. The perfect blend of ambience and great food, at The Belstead.

A showcase of antiques and a friendly environment which will allow guests to relive the Good Old Days, The Vintage Kitchen is a multi cuisine cafe with a Vintage twist. If you want food and fun under same roof, this is the place you are looking for.

This is the right place to make memories which can be cherished forever , also a place where one can indulge themselves into melodious songs and delightful delicacies.

Toss



Ispahani Centre, Nungambakkam, Chennai 600034

Toss (cafe & Lounge) is a very beautiful casual dining restaurant located in Ispahani Centre, Nungambakkam, Central Chennai.

The elegant and rustic decor of Toss makes it the perfect place to chill and relax with your friends and loved ones over great food. The restaurant offers a variety of Chinese and Continental delicacies.

The economical menu and the nice ambiance makes it a perfect place to dine in for every age-group.
